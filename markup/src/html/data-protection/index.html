3. Data We Collect and Purpose of Processing

a) Access Data (Server Logs)

Each time you access our website, your browser automatically transmits data to our server. This includes:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL
  • Date and time of the request

This data is stored indefinitely unless a deletion request is made.

b) Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze user behavior on our site. You can prevent data collection by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

c) Google Maps

Google Maps is integrated to display maps. Your IP address may be transmitted to Google. For more details, see Google’s Privacy Policy.

d) Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to ensure a consistent design. Your browser may retrieve fonts from Google’s servers.

e) jQuery

We use the jQuery framework via Google’s CDN, which may involve the transmission of your IP address.

f) Contact Form

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address (mandatory)

This data is stored indefinitely unless you request its deletion.

g) Newsletter Subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for functionality and analytics. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact info@shvedko.dev.

6. Data Security

We take measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Legal Compliance
